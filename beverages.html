Beer Margaritas

A beer margarita is simply a margarita served with beer. The margarita itself is a classic alcoholic drink on the list of International Bartender Association’s IBA official cocktails.

Ingredients:

  • 1 (12 fluid ounce) can frozen limeade concentrate
  • 12 fluid ounces tequila
  • 12 fluid ounces water, or more as needed
  • 12 fluid ounces beer
  • Ice
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges

Instructions:

  1. Pour limeade concentrate, tequila, water, and beer into a large pitcher. Stir until limeade has melted.

  2. Add plenty of ice to the pitcher and garnish with lime wedges. Adjust with additional water if needed.

  3. Enjoy!

Strawberry Smoothie

This strawberry smoothie recipe blends strawberries with milk, yogurt, sugar, and vanilla for an icy-cold treat. You'll love it!

Ingredients:

  • 8 strawberries, hulled
  • ½ cup skim milk
  • ½ cup plain yogurt
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 6 cubes ice, crushed

Instructions:

  1. Combine strawberries, milk, yogurt, sugar, and vanilla in a blender. Add ice and blend until smooth and creamy.

  2. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.

Creamy Hot Chocolate

Keep some condensed milk on hand for cold days, and whip up a batch of this rich hot chocolate warm your loved ones.

Ingredients:

  • 1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND® Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • ½ cup unsweetened cocoa
  • 1 ½ teaspoons vanilla extract
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • 6 ½ cups hot water
  • 3 Mini marshmallows

Instructions:

  1. Put in large saucepan over medium heat, combine sweetened condensed milk, cocoa, vanilla and salt; mix well. Slowly stir in water. Heat through, stirring occasionally. Do not boil. Top with marshmallows, (optional). Store covered in refrigerator.

  2. In 2-quart glass measure, combine all ingredients except marshmallows. Microwave on 100% power (HIGH) 8 to 10 minutes, stirring every 3 minutes. Top with marshmallows if desired. Store covered in refrigerator.

  3. Enjoy!

Sangria

The best sangria I've been able to hone to perfection for summer parties! I like to use a fruity Spanish red wine, such as Garnacha.

Ingredients

  • 1 (750 milliliter) bottle dry red wine
  • ½ cup brandy
  • ½ cup triple sec
  • ⅓ cup frozen lemonade concentrate
  • ⅓ cup orange juice
  • ¼ cup lemon juice
  • ¼ cup white sugar (Optional)
  • 1 medium orange, sliced into rounds
  • 1 medium lemon, sliced into rounds
  • 1 medium lime, sliced into rounds
  • 8 maraschino cherries
  • 2 cups carbonated water (Optional)

Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients.

  2. Mix together red wine, brandy, triple sec, lemonade concentrate, orange juice, lemon juice, and sugar in a bowl. Add orange, lemon, and lime slices and maraschino cherries.

  3. Serve immediately over ice, or refrigerate 8 hours to overnight for best flavor. For a fizzy sangria, add club soda just before serving.

  4. Enjoy!
